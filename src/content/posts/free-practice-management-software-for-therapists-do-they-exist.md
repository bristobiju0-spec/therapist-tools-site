## What you actually get with free practice management software

Free tools fall into three buckets:

1. **Student or charity licences** – SimplePractice Student Plan (5 clients max), WriteUpp UK charity (≤10 clients/mo).  
2. **Open-source** – OpenEMR, OscarEMR. You host them yourself; no vendor support.  
3. **Discontinued grandfathered plans** – TheraNest Legacy Free ended in 2019; new users can’t join.

All free tiers share common limits: no ERA/EFT insurance, no telehealth, capped clients, and you handle your own HIPAA/GDPR compliance if you self-host.

## FAQ

### Is there a completely free therapy EHR that supports insurance?
No. Every free option skips ERA/EFT. You’ll still log into payer portals to download remittance, which eats more time than the US $39 you’d save.

### What features are missing in free counselling practice management software UK versions?
Free UK licences (WriteUpp charity, open-source) lack NHS Read codes and automated GDPR data-retention rules. You must set those manually.

### Are free platforms HIPAA / GDPR / PIPEDA compliant?
They *can* be, but open-source puts the compliance burden on you. Hosted free tiers (student plans) are compliant, yet cap clients.

### When should I upgrade from a free plan?
Upgrade when you hit the client limit, need telehealth, or spend more than an hour a week on manual billing. At US $39, one reclaimed hour pays for itself.
