2. **Built-in, unlimited telehealth** with group support and automatic note creation—no add-on fee, no third-party integration.
3. **Deep US insurance billing**: payer-specific claim scrubbing, ERA auto-post, batch secondary claims, integrated payments.
4. **Live US-based phone support** included; average wait under a minute, 7 am–7 pm ET.

## Cons: the friction points

1. **No native mobile app**—you’ll rely on browser tabs on your phone.
2. **Pricing is per clinician only**; no low-volume discount; add-on fee after 50 appointment reminders per month.
3. **Client portal is minimal**: no self-scheduling, no secure messaging—clients still email or call to book.
4. **ICD-10 only**; no DSM-5 crosswalk; no SNOMED support; English interface only.
5. **Banking and merchant account setup is US-centric**; no direct debit for UK BACS, AU PAYID, CA EFT.
6. **Time-zone handling for AU/CA daylight-saving** can require manual tweaks.
7. **Reporting exports to CSV/PDF only**; no open API for plugging into Power BI or other analytics.
8. **No integrated payroll or accountant portal**—you’ll still run QuickBooks/Xero separately.

## Best for: US solo therapists who live in notes

TherapyNotes fits you if:
- You see 15–30 clients a week and want the fastest note-writing workflow.
- You bill US insurance and need ERA auto-post and claim scrubbing.
- You run telehealth sessions and don’t want to pay Zoom on top.
- You’re comfortable using a laptop or tablet browser instead of a phone app.
- You don’t need clients to self-schedule or message you inside the portal.

If you practise in the UK, Australia, or Canada, expect extra manual steps for VAT/GST, Medicare, or provincial billing, and no local data-residency option.

## FAQ

### Is TherapyNotes HIPAA compliant for US therapists?
Yes. All data stay on US servers, the company signs a BAA, and the platform meets HIPAA, P-HI, and PIPEDA standards.

### Can clients schedule their own appointments inside TherapyNotes?
No. The portal only allows intake-form completion and card-on-file entry; all scheduling still goes through the clinician or reception staff.

### Does TherapyNotes integrate with QuickBooks or Xero?
There is no direct API. You can export payment and invoice CSV files and import them manually into your accounting package.

### How much does TherapyNotes cost per month for a part-time solo practice?
The fixed price is $49 USD per month regardless of client volume, so a part-time practice pays the same as a full-time one.

### Is there a TherapyNotes mobile app for iPhone or Android?
No. You access the system through a mobile-responsive browser site; offline drafts are not supported.
