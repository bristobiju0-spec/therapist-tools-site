## SimplePractice

### Pricing  
Starter US$39, Essential US$59, Plus US$79. Telehealth is included on Essential and up; on Starter it is a $15 add-on.

### Core features  
- Full client portal (scheduling, intake, secure messaging, auto-billing)  
- Native iOS & Android apps that mirror the web view  
- In-house clearinghouse for US/CA; Stripe integrations for UK/AU  
- 100+ editable progress-note templates and treatment-plans  
- HIPAA-compliant telehealth with no time limits  

### Pros  
- Only option that lets you keep client data in the EU (Frankfurt) or Australia (Sydney) for GDPR compliance  
- Automated eligibility checks across 1,600+ US/CA payers  
- Google Calendar, Zapier, Stripe, Full Slate, Slack integrations  
- ERA/EFT enrollment and CAQH integration built in  

### Cons  
- Price jumps 30% once you add telehealth or insurance tools  
- No phone support on Starter or Essential—chat and email only  
- Learning curve is steeper; expect to spend a weekend setting up templates  

### Best for  
Solo therapists who plan to add clinicians later or need non-US data residency.

## TherapyNotes

### Pricing  
Flat US$49 for one clinician; each additional clinician is +US$30.

### Core features  
- 200+ note templates pre-loaded with DSM-5 criteria  
- Built-in supervisor sign-off for interns & associates  
- Unlimited document storage and custom intake forms  
- HIPAA-compliant telehealth (browser-based)  

### Pros  
- Fastest note-writing workflow; auto-pulls treatment-plan goals  
- Live US-based phone support on every plan  
- Lowest total cost if you file insurance  
- ERA for Medicare, Medicaid, and major commercial payers  

### Cons  
- No native mobile app; browser on phone works but feels cramped  
- Client portal does not allow clients to schedule or start telehealth in the same window  
- Data hosted in US-East only—UK/EU practices need extra GDPR paperwork  

### Best for  
Solo clinicians who live inside their documentation and want the cheapest reliable insurance billing.

## TheraNest

### Pricing  
US$38 solo; US$48 first clinician + US$28 each additional.

### Core features  
- Simple calendar, notes, and credit-card processing  
- Unlimited non-clinical staff logins (billing assistant, VA)  
- HIPAA-compliant telehealth (browser)  

### Pros  
- Cheapest month-to-month plan with no setup fee  
- Interface is light and fast—good for part-time or tech-averse therapists  
- Group practice scaling is the least expensive of the three  

### Cons  
- Product is in maintenance mode; new features roll into Therapy Brands’ unified platform instead  
- No local data hosting outside the US  
- PHI can’t be edited after 24-hour lock without support ticket  
- No Zapier, no two-way Google Calendar sync  

### Best for  
Cash-pay, part-time therapists who want the lowest possible bill and don’t mind missing bells and whistles.
