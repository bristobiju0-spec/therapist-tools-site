## FAQ

**Does a superbill need a diagnosis code?**  
Yes. Insurers require an ICD-10 code that matches the date of service; both programs pull it automatically from your note.

**What is the difference between a superbill and a CMS 1500?**  
A superbill is an itemized receipt the client submits; a CMS 1500 is a claim form the provider usually submits electronically. Use a superbill when you are out-of-network and the client pays you up front.

**Can I create a superbill in SimplePractice for free?**  
During the 30-day trial you can generate unlimited superbills without entering a credit card.

**How long does insurance take to reimburse a superbill?**  
Most commercial plans process out-of-network claims in 14–30 days if the form is complete; missing NPI or CPT can double that time.

**What happens if the client loses the superbill?**  
Log back in, re-print or e-mail another copy; both platforms store the PDF indefinitely.
