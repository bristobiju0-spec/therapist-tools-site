## FAQ

### What is the easiest website builder for therapists with no tech skills?  
Squarespace. The editor is visual, support is 24/7, and you never touch code.

### Can I make a Wix site HIPAA compliant without the most expensive plan?  
No—you need the Business Elite tier plus the Wix Health app for HIPAA forms.

### Is WordPress safe for private-practice therapy websites?  
Yes, but only if you choose a HIPAA-compliant host and use plugins designed for HIPAA, such as WPForms HIPAA or Amelia on encrypted servers.

### Which builder is best for UK and Australian therapists needing GDPR and cookie consent?  
Squarespace and Wix both include GDPR banners; WordPress needs a plugin like Complianz.

### How much does it really cost to run a therapy website on WordPress per year?  
Expect roughly $60–$300 for hosting + $0–$200 for premium themes/plugins—still cheaper than Wix Business Elite after year one.
