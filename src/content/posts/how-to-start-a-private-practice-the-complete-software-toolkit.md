## 8. FAQ

### Do I need an EHR before I see my first client?
Legally no, but you must have HIPAA-secure intake, note, and storage systems. SimplePractice offers a 30-day free trial that covers all three.

### Can I use Headway and SimplePractice together?
Yes. Many clinicians book Headway clients for the first 6–12 months, then export PDF notes and migrate to SimplePractice once their private-pay schedule is full.

### Which software handles CAQH credentialing?
Neither SimplePractice nor TherapyNotes submits CAQH for you; they auto-populate the CAQH forms. Headway submits CAQH on your behalf as part of their onboarding.

### Is telehealth included in TherapyNotes?
Telehealth is a $10/mo add-on that uses doxy.me’s HIPAA-secure video. SimplePractice includes it in the $69 plan.

### Can I bill insurance myself with SimplePractice?
Yes. SimplePractice generates CMS-1500s, submits electronically, and posts ERAs. Starter plan users can purchase claims credits à la carte.
